2% Milk Build Guide

  • Put switches into top of the case with led slots pointed away from the port
  • Solder headers to milk PCB with the 40% logo and the short pins on the same side
  • (Optional) Solder 4cm wire to each of the RGB pins
  • Put PCB and solder switches to it (make sure that the orientation is correct)
  • Solder Pro Micro to headers (I recommend that you only solder these headers as they are the only ones that are used)
  • (Optional) Trim headers on Pro Micro
  • (Optional) Solder RGB Led to wires and attach to bottom of Pro Micro
  • Program with QMK and enjoy
